mONSTER mASH bASH

Our first annual 'Monster Mash Bash 2016 - Adult Halloween Party' saw 95 APV residents pre-registered as particpants online for this awesome, spooky-fun event! APV residents mysteriously appeared out of the fog and into the lavishly-decorated, haunted gymnasium in every conceivable genre and color of costumes imagineable! Participants were expertly dished up a delectible spaghetti and meatball dinner by our courteous and dedicated LIFESTYLE staff members, who were extremely attentive to the needs of each participant in attendance!

DJ Bobby kept the whole outrageously fun party moving at an upbeat pace as he deftly spun up both the new and 'old school' dance hits. DJ Bobby got the whole house of party people up on their feet for the most incredible extended sets of group line-dancing, including the 'Nay Nay Song', 'The Wabble Wabble Dance' and the 'Electric Slide Cha-Cha'!  Carmen Otero and her handsome husband delighted everyone in attendance as they cleared the floor by performing a very awe-inspiring Salsa couples dance!

Next, Jazz Cannon, APV Lifestyle Director, and DJ Bobby, were the party's cheerleading dynamic duo as they involved every particpant in wacky-fun, interactive Halloween contests and games, like the 'Balloon Elbow Pass/Sit Pop', 'Let's Make A Deal' and 'Circle Dance Spotlight'! Zenobid P. (APV Homeowner), dressed up as an adorably shy toddler, was awarded a $25 restaurant gift card as a prize for winning the 'Let's Make A Deal Contest' by amazingly having every crazy item in her purse that our judges asked for!

Our posh Monster Mash Bash 'Grand Finale' was our 'Red Carpet Monster Runway Show', which involved all costumed party particpants strutting their stuff on the spotlit runway at center court to the sounds of Rue Paul's 'Super Model' song! There was a smile on every face and laughter on the lips of all present as each particpant played out the theme of their costume character upon the red carpet. One of the most hilarious red-carpet antics came from the resident portraying Van Gogh's character from his painting 'Scream' and his buddy, who played his hapless victim whom he tapped on the shoulder causing him to feign fainting just before the 'Scream' character drug his limp body by one hand from the red carpet and into the crowd of cheering party-goers!

The APV LIFESTYLE Department wishes to thank all residents in attendance at this exciting event and encourage those who were unable to attend this event to join us this upcoming Saturday, October 29th on the APV Campus beginning at 5:30pm until 7:00pm for the 'Kids Pumpkinfest 2016 Trunk or Treat' and, beginning at 7:00pm until 10:00pm, the 'Zombie Apocalypse Haunted House'...
